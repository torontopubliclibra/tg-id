About TG I.D.

TG I.D. is an online resource collecting information on how to update legal names, gender markers, and identity documents as a trans, non-binary, Two-Spirit, or gender non-conforming individual living in Ontario, Canada.

The project is run by Dana Rosamund Teagleâ€”a designer, web/software developer, and trans woman based in Tkaronto (Toronto), Ontario. Dana has led workshops on name and gender marker changes since 2021.

The level of bureaucracy currently in place across the country for these vital services and processes makes them fundamentally inaccessible. We believe that communities coming together to share our experiences and resources with one another is an act of resistance, and that mentality is embedded in our approach.

This site does not in any way provide legal advice. If you feel unsure about any information provided, please cross-reference the official sources linked at the bottom of each page, or contact the webmaster with your concerns. Should you require legal info or resources, we recommend checking out the JusticeTrans website.
