About TG I.D.

TG I.D. is an online resource collecting information on how to update legal names, gender markers, and identity documents in Ontario, Canada.

The level of bureaucracy currently in place makes these vital processes fundamentally inaccessible. TG I.D. aims to empower individuals by providing clear and accessible information and resources.

Please note however that this site does not provide legal advice. If you have questions or concerns about any information here, cross-reference the official sources linked at the bottom of each page, and feel free to contact the webmaster. For legal information or resources, we recommend visiting the JusticeTrans website.
